A Foundational Framework

Why indoor soccer needs its own analytical vocabulary, and the first version of an indoor-calibrated expected-goals model. Eight chapters covering possession structure, shot quality in confined space, and the historical context for why indoor analytics has lagged every other major sport.

II
Published · 2026

The Playoff Paradox

A retrospective of the 2026 Ron Newman Cup and the first published Pythagorean expectation for the MASL. Derives the league's exponent (N = 2.76) from 149 team-seasons and explains, via 100,000 Monte Carlo simulations, why the regular-season leader has won the championship just 30 percent of the time.

8 chapters · Pythagorean derivation · Bracket Monte Carlo · ~40 min
